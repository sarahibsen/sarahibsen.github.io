Seed Sync

Collaborative UX/UI App Design for Community Gardening

My Core Responsibilities

  • UX Research Lead: Led all user interviews and synthesized data into actionable statistics.
  • UI Design: Designed the Login, Starter/Onboarding, and Goals tracking pages.
  • Prototyping: Built high-fidelity mockups to demonstrate user flow.

The Process

Seed Sync was a group effort where I spearheaded the research phase. By conducting interviews with local gardeners, I pulled statistical insights that shaped our app's features. I focused on making the "onboarding" experience seamless, ensuring users felt motivated by the "Goals" page from the moment they logged in.
