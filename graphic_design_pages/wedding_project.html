The All-In-One Wedding Experience

Creative Direction, Printmaking, and 3D Visualization

The Challenge

For this project, I acted as a holistic designer for an engaged couple, handling everything from initial aesthetic consultations to physical production and 3D spatial planning. The goal was to create a cohesive, personalized brand for their special day.

Digital & Strategy

  • Consultation: Interviewed the couple to define favorite colors and aesthetics.
  • Photography: Conducted, edited, and produced the engagement photoshoot.
  • Registry: Designed and launched a custom wedding website.
  • 3D Modeling: Used Blender 3D to digitally prototype the venue layout.

Physical & Craft

  • Handmade Paper: Created RSVP letters using hand-pressed paper with flower petals.
  • Printmaking: Hand-printed the RSVP suite for a tactile feel.
  • Gold Foil: Created table placement cards using golden heat signatures for each name.
  • Digital/Print: Designed cohesive digital Save the Dates.

Project Takeaway

This project pushed my skills beyond 2D design. By combining logistics (contacting caterers), 3D environment design (Blender), and traditional craft (handmade paper), I was able to provide a full-service experience that ensured every touchpoint of the wedding was on-brand.
