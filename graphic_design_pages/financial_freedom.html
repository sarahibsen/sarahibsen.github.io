Future Forward School Supplies

A one-stop resource for college student success.

Role

UI/UX Designer & Researcher

Tools

Figma, Collaborative Design

Timeline

4 Weeks

The Challenge

College students often struggle to find affordable housing and supplies across fragmented websites. Our goal was to create "Future Forward," a unified platform that streamlines the search for scholarships and essential resources.

Interactive Prototype

Note: You can interact with the slides above to see our full presentation and design logic.

Design Logic

We focused on a "Mobile-First" approach, ensuring that students on the go could quickly filter through housing listings and scholarship deadlines.
